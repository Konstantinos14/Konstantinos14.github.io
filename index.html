Research Interests

🧠

Neurolinguistics

Investigating the neural substrates of language processing using EEG, fMRI, and electrophysiology. Particular interest in event-related potentials (N400, P600) and what they reveal about syntactic and semantic processing.

EEG/ERP fMRI Syntax N400
⚙️

Computational Linguistics

Building and evaluating computational models of language comprehension and production. Interested in whether large language models capture anything meaningful about human linguistic cognition.

NLP Language Models Parsing Python
💡

Cognitive Science

Exploring the broader cognitive architecture underlying language — memory, attention, executive function — and how language interacts with other cognitive systems during development and in clinical populations.

Working Memory Bilingualism Development Aphasia
